Thesis

STRUCTURAL HEALTH MONITORING SYSTEM



This project described a method to examine the dynamic behaviours of buildings under the external factors. Information about the characteristic and health of structuresis provided to users via short-time measurements taken from the structures. To take these measurements, four processors with accelerometer and communication modules were installed in the corners of the top floor of the buildings. The information received from from the four processors was transmitted to a central processor via wireless communication. The collected data was transferred to the Matlab in the central processor for signal processing in order to evaluate this information and provide actionable insights. FFT (Fast Fourier Transform) signal processing and spectrum analysis were performed. The required modal frequencies are displayed in the digital environment for the user can understand.
